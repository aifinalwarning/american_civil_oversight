Pro Tips for Maximum Impact

1

Be Specific & Personal

Share your personal story and how the issue affects you directly. Generic messages are less impactful than authentic experiences.

2

Phone Calls Win

A brief, polite phone call is often 10x more effective than email. Call during business hours and keep it under 2 minutes.

3

Reference Bill Numbers

Always mention the specific bill number (H.R.1234 or S.567). This helps staff route your feedback to the right team.

4

Comment Early

Submit public comments early in the comment period. Use facts, cite sources, and suggest specific alternatives when opposing something.

5

Follow Up

Civic engagement is ongoing. Follow up on concerns, attend town halls, and maintain relationships with your representatives' offices.

6

Organize Together

Collective action amplifies your voice. Join groups, coordinate messaging, and show up together. Numbers matter to legislators.
